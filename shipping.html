Shipping Policy

Our Shipping Policy outlines the shipping methods, rates, and delivery times for orders placed on GreenMorph.

1. Shipping Methods

We offer various shipping methods based on your location, including standard and expedited shipping options.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on the delivery address and the weight of the items purchased.

3. Delivery Time

Delivery times vary depending on the shipping method chosen. Typical delivery times range from 5 to 10 business days for domestic shipments.

4. International Shipping

We currently do not offer international shipping. We are working on expanding our reach globally.

If you have any further questions about shipping, please feel free to contact us.
